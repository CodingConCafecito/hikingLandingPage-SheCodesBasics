A brief introduction to

H I K I N G

With over 80,000 hiking trails to explore in the United States alone, the opportunities for exploration and adventure are plentiful.

Health Benefits

🫀

Cardiovascular

Hiking is an great cardio exercise that will get your blood flowing and strengthen your heart.

😌

Mood

According to a Stanford University research study, taking a walk in nature can decrease ruminating thoughts and thus protect your mental health.

💪🏾

Strength

Going on a hike improves bone and muscle density. Having strong bones and muscles significantly decreases your risk of injuries.

🧠

Cognitive

There are a plethora of research studies that have found that spending time in nature can help improve our overall brain health, including things like improved memory and focus.

Advice For Beginners

  • 🥾 Start with shorter and less challenging trails.
  • 🥾 Invest in a reliable pair of hiking shoes.
  • 🥾 Bring a friend!

Additional Hiking Tips

  • 🌻 Let someone know where you are going.
  • 🌻 Protect yourself from the elements.
  • 🌻 Bring water and snacks.

Reminder: There is no wrong way to hike, what is important is having fun and enjoying the experience!
